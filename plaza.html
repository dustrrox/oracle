Welcome, weary traveller! To the
land where Apollo himself decides
your fate!
The steps of this mighty pilgrimage
have been traversed by merchants
and Kings alike!

Twas at this great Temple of Apollo,
where our powerful Pythia,
known as the Oracle of Delphi,
assured Alexander the Great of his
victories and Socrates was declared
the wisest of all men

Our Oracle meets with her fated petitioners
on the 7th day after each new moon,
only 9 times a year!
You, weary traveller, are a few days early!
